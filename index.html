Every great brand begins with a story. Mine began with curiosity — a deep fascination with how technology can transform how we live, work, and connect. What started as hours of coding and design experiments turned into a lifelong mission: to help medium-sized businesses, brands & startups with a target revenue of £2M–£50M scale and exceed their targets.

Over the years, I’ve worn many hats — developer, consultant, and founder — but the mission has always stayed the same: build systems that make businesses growth simple and scalable.


After graduating with a Web Programming certificate from Moringa School and later refining my craft with a Bachelor’s in Computer Information Systems from Kenya Methodist University, I entered the tech world driven by curiosity and grit. From leading product teams to developing advanced web systems, my journey has been defined by three principles:

  • Innovation — always seeking smarter ways to solve digital challenges.
  • Integrity — building technology that earns trust, not just clicks.
  • Elevation — helping businesses achieve measurable, meaningful growth.

Today, I lead a startup
AlonGithui Commerce Solutions (AGCS) — a tech brand based in the UK that helps lifestyle and retail brands scale online with Shopify, Vue.js, and modern web automation.
